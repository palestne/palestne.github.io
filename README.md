# PvM Rush on Iron (PROi)

## Prologue

The goal of this guide/playthrough/series is reach high level PvM fairly quickly on a fresh iron account. In order to PvM effectively we want to focus on the following:

  - Starting up kingdom, to help upkeep herbs and get construction supplies
  - 85 construction, to set up resetting and teleportation logistics 
  - Max combat skills, to remove stat check from PvM
  - Questing, to unlock key boseses for gear upgrades and slayer tasks
  - Hard diaries + elite Lumbridge/Kourend, to optimize slayer block list and teleports to bosses ie rada 4 to hydra

Maxing will not be the goal of this guide and aside from a select few non combat skills (ie construction for house + bank tp) most will be left at just the level required to do a certain quest/diary (ie fm at 75).

The above list is relatively in sequence. So in order to start up kingdom and level construction we need GP. 

## Season 1: Early Game

### Chapter 1: Solving the cash problem 

To get GP we head to wilderness agility, which will pretty much set up the account for life. 

  - Get 52 agility 

  Once we have that unlocked we'll need an initial cash stack to fund the 150k entrance fee. As such we'll need to do some Wintertodt. 

  - Do Stronghold of Security for 10k
  - Grab clue hunter boots/gloves, boots of lightness 
  - Do Daddy's Home 
  - Grab a fire staff (you may need sell a couple bolts of cloth)
  - Do Children of the Sun for access to axe shop. grab mithril and addy axe
  - Get 50 fm 
  - X Marks the Spot, trek to Wintertodt and grab clue hunter garb on the way

While we're here for fm to an extent (we'll want 75 fm for quest cape) we are more interested the rewards. 

  - Solo Wintertodt till 75 fm

This should give us enough funds to cover the 150k entrance fees at the wilderness course. We'll obviously get a lot of funds from the course itself, but it'll be tricky converting those to cash since we don't have 55 magic for alching yet, and we don't want to raise magic to keep combat level low. 
  
  - Do wilderness course till 80 agility 

With more money we know what to do with we are completed chapter 1, and head to setting up our core dailies.
